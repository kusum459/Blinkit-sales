Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive business intelligence dashboard analyzing sales performance for Blinkit, India's quick-commerce grocery delivery platform. The dashboard consolidates outlet-level sales, product, and store data to support retail decision-making.

Key Metrics

Total Sales: 1.2M
Average Sales: 141k
Number of Items: 8,523
Average Rating: 3.9

Visual Insights

Total Sales by Fat Content: Pie chart showing the split between Low Fat products (776.32k) and Regular products (425.36k).

Fat Content by Outlet: Column chart comparing Low Fat versus Regular product sales across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 outlets.

Total Sales by Item Type: Horizontal bar chart ranking sales across categories such as Fruits and Vegetables, Snack Foods, Household, Frozen Foods, Dairy, Canned goods, and others.

Sales by Outlet Size: Donut chart showing distribution across High, Medium, and Small outlet sizes.

Sales by Outlet Location: Bar chart comparing Tier 1 (336,398), Tier 2 (393,151), and Tier 3 (472,133) sales.

Count of Outlet Type: Column chart showing the breakdown by Grocery Store, Supermarket Type 1, Type 2, and Type 3.

Sum of Outlet Type: Bar chart showing total sales aggregated by store format.

Filter Panel

The dashboard includes filters for Outlet Size (High, Medium, Small), Item Type (Hard Drinks, Health and Hygiene, Household, Meat, Snack Foods, Soft Drinks, and others), and Outlet Location Type (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3), allowing users to drill down into specific segments.
